## üí° CONVERSION PSYCHOLOGY INSIGHTS

### What Sellers Want:
1. **Transparency** - Show fees, process, timelines clearly
2. **Proof** - Testimonials, stats, case studies
3. **Education** - Detailed information to make informed decisions
4. **Trust** - Personal connection and credibility
5. **Value** - Clear ROI and benefits

### Psychological Triggers to Use:
- **Authority:** Stats, experience, team credentials
- **Social Proof:** Testimonials, reviews, success stories
- **Scarcity:** Limited-time consultations, market timing
- **Reciprocity:** Free consultation, resources, education
- **Commitment:** Forms create investment in the process
